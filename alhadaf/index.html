Third Party Inspection

-Physical & Mechanical Testing of construction, equipment and scaffolding. - Controlling and mitigating risk of health and safety.

Training

Includes four different types of courses with major concern to safety, conducted by our firm's engineers in addition to assistance of well reputed, acting safety managers. It includes practical training by safety Supervisors in the field.

Safety Survey & Auditing

1- Contractor’s site Safety plan. 2- Emergency Escape plan. 3- Safety survey, audit reporting for factories and workshops …etc. and for construction sites as well, in relation to verify compliance with local guidelines & international regulations. 4- Equipment evaluation reports for damage & loss evaluation.
