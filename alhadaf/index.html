Inspection Personnel

Our Inspection staff are well qualified besides than our experienced Inspection Engineers who have acquired their professional degrees from highly reputable Universities.

Our Inspection staff are completely updated in BS Standards and Rules & Regulations in order to undertake equipment examinations, to keep abreast of the latest, revised and mandatory requirements.

Impartiality & Independence

Explicit guidance has been documented by AL HADAF for instances where staff experiences risks of undue pressure from any source, internal or external to the organization, which could influence the results of assessment. AL HADAF demonstrates independence to strengthen the confidence of customers by carrying out inspection work impartially and objectively.

Confidentiality


AL HADAF has a documented procedure on its Management System concerning the observance of confidentiality requirements of the customer, by AL HADAF or by any sub-contractors engaged with it, taking into account any relevant legal requirements.
