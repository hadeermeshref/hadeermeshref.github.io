Our Services

Install all required safety equipment

We install all safety equipment required for shops, warehouses, offices, buildings, hotels, workshops and all facilities

safety plans for the Civil Defense

We select and follow up the design and approval of safety plans in the engineering offices approved by the Civil Defense, according to the requirements of the fire alarm and firefighting approved by the Civil Defense for speed of implementation.

Providing all maintenance contracts for facilities

Because we are one of the safety companies approved by the Civil Defense in Riyadh. We provide all maintenance contracts for alarm and firefighting systems for facilities.

Provide all required papers and documents to complete the procedures for the safety site service

Because we are one of the safety companies approved by the Civil Defense in Riyadh, we provide all the required papers and documents to complete the procedures for the safety site service. -Certificate of completion of installation -Electrical wiring safety certificate -Certificate of Achievement for Administrative Control

fire pumps

Fire pumps push water to firefighting systems when needed according to each firefighting system, we supply, install and maintain all fire pumps.

Fire alarm panels

voice evacuation system is designed to issue a notification so that an area crowded with people, such as a work area, for example, is evacuated, in the event of an accident, God forbid, such in order to save lives and not lose them, which is common in many buildings.

Issuance and renewal of municipal and civil defense license

We issue and renew municipal and civil defense licenses, install gas leak detectors and gas arrestors, complete the electronic safety service site and conduct the requirements of the fire alarm and firefighting.

fire alarms

Fire alarms are the primary consideration to avoid fire losses. Saving lives is all we aim for in the event of fires occurring in the building so that it is quickly left before the spread of the fire. This is the primary function of fire alarm devices.

Fire extinguisher

The use of a fire extinguisher is important to put out fires in many places. The validity and follow-up dates must be as stipulated in the code and civil defense requirements

Security camera installation

The use of monitoring systems is very important and one of the requirements of the Ministry of Interior for establishments and stores.
