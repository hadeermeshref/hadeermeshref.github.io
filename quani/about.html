ABOUT US

Quani is a national company specialized in firefighting, safety and equipment and security systems,. It is one of the companies approved by the Civil Defense and other relevant official bodies (Ministry of Interior - Ministry of Commerce - Ministry of Municipal and Rural Affairs and Housing). The company has conducted many projects with the government and private sector and concluded many periodic maintenance contracts. The company also has an elite group of employees, qualified engineers and skilled labor. It also seeks to expand geographically to provide products of high quality and value at a confident and deliberate pace while adopting advanced methodologies and systems to manage and develop work.
